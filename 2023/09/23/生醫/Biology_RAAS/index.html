腎素-血管收縮素-醛固酮系統 (RAAS)

2023/9/23 生醫

The Renin-Angiotensin-Aldosterone System (RAAS)

作用機制：

血管收縮素原(angiotensinogen) --(腎素(酶))–> 血管收縮素

  1. 近腎小球細胞分泌腎素如果：
  • 近腎小球細胞偵測到血壓下降
  • 緻密班偵測濃度下降

血管收縮素依作用位置的反應：

  1. 一級反應
  • 下視丘->口渴
  • 腎上腺皮質->醛固酮分泌
  • 近曲小管->再吸收
  • 腦垂腺後葉->(下視丘產生)釋出血管加壓素(Vasopressin)/抗利尿激素(Antidiuretic Hormone, ADH)分泌
  • 血管收縮
  • 交感神經作用
  1. 二級反應
  • 水(血量)保存
    • 喝水
    • 再吸收(遠曲小管)
    • 抗利尿激素增加水的再吸收(遠曲小管/集尿管)
  • 血壓回升

->恢復恆定

當滲透壓增加

  • 受到神經與激素的控制
  • 滲透壓增加->刺激下視丘滲透壓受器->口渴感(開源)
  • 滲透壓增加->刺激下視丘滲透壓受器->腦垂腺後葉釋出抗利尿激素->增加遠曲小管/集尿管水的通透性(增加水通道蛋白的數量)->增加水分在腎臟的再吸收->排出濃尿
  • 酒精->抑制ADH分泌->尿量增加->輕微脫水->刺激下視丘滲透壓受器->口渴感

腎結構

Per nephron

juxtaglomerular apparatus

JG cells (juxtaglomerular cells) store and secrete renin

  1. Renin is an enzyme that converts angiotensinogen -> angiotensin I
  2. Angiotensin I is converted to angiotensin II by angiotensin-converting enzyme (ACE) in the lungs

Angiotensin II

Direct effects

  • Vasoconstriction
    • Increase blood pressure
  • At glomerulus
    • Constrict the efferent arteriole(入球小動脈)
    • Increase gfr and filtration pressure
  • Proximal convoluted tubule
    • Stimulate the sodium-hydrogen exchanger -> increase Na+ reabsorption/ H+ secretion -> more HCO3- reabsorption

Indirect effects

  • Stimulate the adrenal cortex to secrete aldosterone
    • Increase Na+ reabsorption/Potassium secretion(Principle cells)
    • Increase ATPase activity/H+ secretion(alpha-intercalated cells)
  • Stimulate the anti-diuretic hormone (ADH) secretion
    • Increase water reabsorption
  • Stimulate the hypothalamus to increase thirst

Overall effects

RAAS:

  1. Arterial pressure increase
  2. GFR increase and filtration pressure increase
  3. Na+/H2O reabsorption increase
  4. K+ secretion increase
  5. Contraction of alkalosis

Stimulated by:

  1. Low arterial pressure

    Fast: Constrict -> increase pressure
    Slow: Reabsorbs sodium and water -> increase blood volume -> increase pressure

  2. Low NaCl delivery to macula densa
  3. Increased sympathetic tone
