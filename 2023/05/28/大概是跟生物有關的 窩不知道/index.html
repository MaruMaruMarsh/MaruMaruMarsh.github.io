Membrane Potential

Resting Membrane Potential

  • Voltage difference at rest
    • Approx -70mV

      mV(millivolt):

  • Uneven distribution of ions
2 Things determine or establish the resting m.p.
  • diff in ion concentration
  • diff in membrane permeability
Sodium-potassium pump
  • -1 mV to -3 mV
  • Potassium leak channel move potassium down its concentration gradient(high$\to$low).
    1. it makes outside more positive!
    2. anion can’t leave the cell through this door :(
      ->more negative inside(an electrical gradient generated)
    3. In the same time, Electrical gradient move potassium back to the cell!
    4. if we deal with single ion ($\ce{K+}$)
      when it reached equilibrium potential, result of m.p. will be -90 mV (WOW)
  • Sodium leak channel
    1. establish that conc of the Sodium higher ouside
    2. move Sodium ions down its concentration gradient (move into the cell this time)
    • Notice that chloride ions are also there (they are key ions found in extracellular space)
    1. because chloride are left behind, make the outside more negative
    2. when it reached equilibrium potential, m.p. will be +60 mV

      Wow things:

      • 膜對鉀的滲透性比鈉好，漏的鉀會多一點。
      • 反正最後全部總和起來大概 -70 mV，也就是一般咱們看到的膜電位數值
Nernst equation

$$\boxed{
\ce{E_{ion}=\frac{61}{z}\log({\frac{C_{out}}{C_{in}}})}}
$$
痾用公式算下分別的電位

  • $\displaystyle \ce{E_{Na}=\frac{61}{+1}\log({\frac{145}{15}})=+60mV}$
  • $\displaystyle \ce{E_{K}=\frac{61}{+1}\log({\frac{5}{150}})=-90mV}$
